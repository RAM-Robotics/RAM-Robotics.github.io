Welcome to RAM Robotics

The Home of ARIS

A common failure point of floating offshore wind farms are the dynamic subsea riser cables attached to each turbine. Current methods of inspection are irregular and expensive, with defects only found through catastrophic failure. ARIS, the Autonomous Riser Inspection System, aims to provide regular inspection of these riser cables in a cost-effective manner.
